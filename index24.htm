World Wildlife Fund

2012 - 2014

For two years I was employed by the WWF as a Web Producer. I came aboard just in time for the relaunch of their website www.worldwildlife.org. in a new CMS with a responsive design.

After it's launch I led the development and deployment of corrections to the site via Github, troubleshooting HTML, CSS/SASS and Javascript issues. I also developed and installed new features, interactive modules, and third party plugins on the site.

World Wildlife Fund Project - Earth Hour

2014

One of my projects during my stay at the WWF was an interactive quiz geared towards promoting one of WWF's campaigns to increase awareness on solar energy. It is still online here.

I collaborated with program staff to determine the content of the quiz and then designed and coded it myself using HTML, CSS and JQuery.

The quiz featured animations, different outcomes depending on score and was coded on the backend so that every click and score was tracked with Google Event Tracking. This enabled WWF to determine the popularity of the quiz, which questions the public answered correct or incorrect, and how many shared the quiz and used it's call to action links at the end.

  • What I Used
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JQuery
  • Google Analytics

Gannett

2005 - 2011

I spent almost 7 years at Gannett, one of the largest news media companies in the country.

During my time as a Team Leader in the Digital Production Center I managed the needs of nine news websites. Towards the end of my time there, I was tasked with building the new Gannett corporate website, which remains online today.

During the process I collaborated with Gannett designers to conceptualize, build, and launch the site. I was responsible for design concepts, coding, and it's integration with a Saxotech CMS.

Underground Poetry Spot

2008 - 2012

I designed and created a custom logo, Wordpress website, and marketing materials for a nonprofit poetry organization based in Syracuse N.Y.

The website featured a completely custom theme complete with a homepage carousel, video and audio players, contact forms and a blog that I kept updated with fresh artwork and content.
