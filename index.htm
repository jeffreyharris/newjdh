World Wildlife Fund Project - Earth Hour

2014

During Earth Hour, hundreds of millions of people around the world turn off their lights for one hour to show their commitment to helping the planet.

For Earth Hour 2014 I collaborated with program staff to develop an Earth Hour Signup module. The module allows users to signup for Earth Hour, collecting their information through a Convio API. If the user is on a mobile device, it will ask for their phone number as well for text notifications.

The module was both responsive and visually dynamic. It changed its appearance depending on how many people were signed up and kept a running tally of signups at the top. Every interaction with the module was tracked through Google Analytics Event Tracking so that its performance could be analyzed. In the days leading up to Earth Hour 2014, this module captured thousands of signups and valuable information for further fund raising efforts.

  • What I Used

  • CSS / SASS
  • Javascript / JQuery
  • Convio API

World Wildlife Fund Project - Solar Energy Quiz

2013

Another project of mine at the WWF was an interactive quiz geared towards promoting WWF's campaign to increase awareness on solar energy. You can still experience it yourself here.

I collaborated with program staff to determine the content of the quiz. I then planned the UX and design of the quiz and coded it using HTML, CSS and JavaScript / JQuery.

The quiz is completely responsive and features animations, different outcomes depending on your score, and was coded so that every click and score was tracked with Google Event Tracking. This enabled WWF to determine the popularity of the quiz, which questions the public answered correct or incorrect, and how many shared the quiz or used its call to action links upon completion. Upon launch, this quiz was taken by tens of thousands of WWF supporters.
